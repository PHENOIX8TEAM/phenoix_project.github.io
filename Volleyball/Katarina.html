Katarina Lazović

a Serbian volleyball player, playing as wing spiker.

Katarina Lazović (Serbian Cyrillic: Катарина Лазовић; born 12 September 1999) is a Serbian volleyball player, playing as wing spiker.[1] She had played for OK Vizura Belgrade before continuing an international career in Poland in 2019 for ŁKS Łódź.[2][3] From 2021 she joined Italian club Pallavolo Monza.[4][5] She is a member of the Serbia women's national volleyball team. She participated at the 2019 Women's European Volleyball Championship and winning gold medal.
